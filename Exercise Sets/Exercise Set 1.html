How to cook Chicken Adobo

  1. Ingredients
    • Chicken
    • Onions
    • Garlic
    • Sugar to taste
    • Soy Sauce to taste
    • Vinegar to taste
    • Black pepper to taste
    • Water
  2. Preparation
    1. The Marinade
      1. Put the chicken in a tupperware
      2. Add soy sauce and sugar
    2. How to Cook
      1. Sear the chicken in a hot pan.
      2. Remove the chicken from the pan to a different container
      3. The crust on the pan from searing will be very tasty. Sautee garlic and onions in it.
      4. Add the soy sauce and a little bit of water to deglaze the pan
      5. Put the chicken back in and add the other seasonings to taste.
      6. Let the adobo simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Serving
    • Serve with rice
    • Add garnish and some toasted garlic
